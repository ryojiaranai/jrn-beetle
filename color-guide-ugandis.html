Ugandis Flower Beetle

Mecynorrhina torquata ugandensis — One of the beetles in our collection

Quick Info

  • Scientific Name: Mecynorrhina torquata ugandensis
  • Family: Scarabaeidae (Flower Chafers)
  • Origin: Uganda & Democratic Republic of Congo
  • Size: Males 50–85mm, Females 45–60mm

One of the largest flower beetles in the world. Males sport an impressive frontal horn used for wrestling rivals — always fun to watch!

Our Morphs

Red
Warm metallic red-orange coloration. A striking and popular color form of this giant flower beetle.
