Derbyana Flower Beetle

Dicronorhina derbyana — One of the beetles in our collection

Quick Info

Scientific Name: Dicronorhina derbyana
Family: Scarabaeidae (Flower Chafers)
Origin: Sub-Saharan Africa
Size: Males 40–50mm, Females 35–38mm
Fun fact: Males have a distinctive T-shaped flat horn

Our Morphs

These are the color forms we currently keep at JRN BEETLE.

Purple
Dark purple metallic coloration. One of the more striking subspecies/color forms.
Blue
Metallic blue form. A beautiful and eye-catching color variation.

Life Cycle

How I Raise Larvae

Bulk Raising
I raise Derbyana larvae in bulk — no need to separate them individually.

L1–L2: 20 larvae in a 5-litre container
L3: 5–10 larvae in a 5-litre container

Substrate change: Every 3–5 weeks
